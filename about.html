ABOUT ANA HUANG

Born on March 7th 1991 to Chinese expat parents, Ana Huang (ANN-uh Hw-ON-g) is a Chinese-American new-adult fiction, romance author.

Huang started as a Wattpad author, encouraged my her mother to write novels to improve her English. She later on self-published her Twisted Series on tiktok during the Covid pandemic, which gained notable traction, and was later on republished by a renowned publisher, Bloom Books, in 2022

Her work has sold over 25 million copies worldwide and has been translated into thirty-four languages. In 2024, she was the 4th highest selling BookTok author with 1, 474, 194 total sales. She's appears on Cosmopolitan, Elle Magazine, Good Morning America, the Kelly Clarkson Show, the Drew Barrymore Show, the Washington Post, and more.
